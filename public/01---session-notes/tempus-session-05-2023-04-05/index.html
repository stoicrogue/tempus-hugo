Strong Start#

  • We left off in Tannis after the party woke to the screams of villagers and a veil of magical darkness which prevents the players from seeing more than 20 ft in any direction
    • They fought off one Spawn that grabbed Slumpet after watching another Spawn kill a villager
  • After stopping in to check on [[Lerissa]] at the bar, the party started going to the church.
    • They find the church casting light through its stained glass windows that can be seen from the bottom of the hill.
    • As the party approaches, they see two Spawns scratching at the church walls, unable to get through. They can fight the Spawn or try to find a way into the church.

Deadly Encounter Benchmark#

Calculate the deadly encounter benchmark by adding together all character levels and dividing by 2 if they’re 5th level or above or divide by 4 if they’re 1st to 4th level. An encounter may be deadly if the sum total of monster challenge ratings is greater than half the sum total of character levels or one quarter of character levels if the characters are 4th level or below.
