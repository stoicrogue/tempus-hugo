# Trius - Paladin, Oath of the Ancients

> [!tip] AC 20 | HP 52 | Speed 30 ft. | Init +0
> Passive Perception 14 | Darkvision 60 ft.
> Immune: disease, magical sleep | Advantage vs being charmed


> [!info] AURA (passive ability)
> **Aura of Protection:** while conscious, you + all friendly creatures within 10 ft gain +2 to ALL saving throws.

## Primary Attack (x2)
- Moonblade > Melee Weapon Attack: 
	- To Hit: d20+8, reach 5 ft, 1 target
	- Damage: 1d8+5 slashing

## Core Turn Loop

### Round 1
Pick ONE concentration plan:
- A) Bless (party accuracy + saves) — best “team fight” opener
- B) Shield of Faith (+2 AC) - makes you harder to hit
Then: move in + Attack twice with Moonblade. Smite when it matters.

### Round 2+
- Attack twice every turn. 
- Spend spell slots on Divine Smite for burst damage.
- Use Misty Step / Fey Step to teleport 30ft to chase enemy or escape danger.

## Resource Tracker
- Spell Slots: 1st (4), 2nd (2)
- Lay on Hands: 30 HP per long rest (Action), heals allies
- Channel Divinity: 1 per short rest (Action). DC 13
- Divine Sense: 3 per long rest (Action)

## SIGNATURE “PANIC BUTTONS”
- Misty Step (BA): teleport to reposition (always prepared).
- Fey Step (BA, 1/short rest): teleport 30 ft (plus a seasonal rider).
- Nature’s Wrath (Channel Divinity, Action): restrain a target in melee range (DC 13). 
- Lay on Hands (Action): pick someone up / stabilize / big heal.

## DIVINE SMITE (your damage spike)
When you hit with a melee weapon attack, spend a spell slot to add:
- +2d8 radiant (1st level slot)
- +3d8 radiant (2nd level slot)
***(+1d8 more vs undead)***

## COMBAT SPELL “SHORTLIST”

### Bonus Action
- Wrathful Smite (Conc): add fear pressure (Wis save DC 13).
- Shield of Faith (Conc): +2 AC.
- Misty Step: teleport.

### Action
- Bless (Conc): team-wide power boost.
- Command: force “Drop”, “Grovel”, etc. (Wis DC 13).
- Aid (no Conc): +5 max HP (8 hours).
- Moonbeam (Conc): area control + radiant damage (always prepared).
- CANTRIPS (from Blessed Warrior)
	- Word of Radiance (CON save DC 13, close-range AoE).
	- Spare the Dying (stabilize).

> [!info] NOTE ON CONCENTRATION
> You can only concentrate on ONE spell at a time (Bless OR Shield of Faith OR Moonbeam OR a Smite spell, etc.).
