All the characters in the Tempus campaign, organized into four categories:

- **Main Party** -- The player characters and quick-reference sheets for their stats and abilities.
- **Allies** -- Friendly NPCs the party has encountered along the way, from Elara Sunforge to the flameskull Francis.
- **Adversaries** -- The villains and antagonists, including stat blocks for major threats like the Weaver and Manus.
- **Monsters** -- Creature stat blocks for everything the party has fought, from Shadows and Darklings to an Ancient Shadow Dragon.
