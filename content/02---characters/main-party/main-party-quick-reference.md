## Francis, the Flameskull

> AC/DC 13 | HP 40 (25–65) | Atk/Prof +5/+2 | DPR 26 | # Atks 2 | Dmg 13 (3d6 + 3)

- **Fire Ray Barrage:** Two Fire Ray attacks per turn; strong ranged pressure for its CR.
- **Fire Ray:** Melee or ranged (60 ft.); fire damage.
- **Spellcasting (Limited):** 2/day Magic Missile (2nd), 1/day Fireball (burst spike).
- **Magic Resistance:** Advantage on saves vs spells and magical effects.
- **Damage Immunities:** Fire, necrotic, poison
- **Condition Immunities:** Charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, prone
- **Mobile Artillery:** Fly 40 ft. (hover)
